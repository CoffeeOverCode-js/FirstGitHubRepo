Who am I?
My name is Ryan Fredericks, and I was born on May 20, 2005, in Cape Town, South Africa. I have many passions and hobbies in life, including sports-shooting, playing the piano, fishing, and technology. When I am passionate about something, I put in every ounce of effort possible. For instance, during my sports-shooting training, I would spend most Saturdays at the club from 7 am to 4 pm. Although it was tiring, it was worth it in the end because I managed to win many club tournaments and was selected to compete in the 2022 junior nationals for .22 LR sports-shooting.

How it all started.
Growing up with both of my parents working as police officials for the government, I developed a passion for sports-shooting. When I was between the ages of 9 and 12, my dad joined a club called SA Jagters, and I started attending annual junior camps where I learned about survival techniques and gun safety. This was the start of my competitive journey in sports-shooting.

Fast forward to 2020, and I have grown immensely as a shooter, becoming one of the best juniors in long gun shoots. We decided to join another club in False Bay, which was more convenient for us since it was closer than our previous club. Although we continued to participate in tournaments at our other club, we practiced at the False Bay club.

During my time at the False Bay club, I had the opportunity to meet one of my dad's colleagues, who is now my instructor. He introduced me to the 9mm handgun, and after seeing promise in me, he invested even more time and money in my training. Thanks to his guidance, I earned a Distinguished Master certificate in 9mm handgun shooting, which requires an average score of 94-97%.

Over the next two years, I continued to excel in handgun shoots and won four more Distinguished Master certificates. In 2022, I was selected to compete in the nationals for .22LR sports-shooting in Welkom, Johannesburg, and in 2023, I achieved my greatest accomplishment: the High Master certificate for .22LR sports-shooting. I am extremely proud of myself for all the hard work and countless hours I have put into sports-shooting.
